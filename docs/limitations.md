# Limitations

- The current prototype does not prove full real-time internet research because search and retrieval are not implemented.
- LLM-generated themes may be incomplete, outdated or biased.
- Outputs require source grounding before they are used for business decisions.
- Local models may hallucinate when asked to summarise feedback without retrieved evidence.
- Planned citations must be verified against the retrieved source text before publication.
- Model-generated source counts in the current response schema are estimates, not counts of documents actually retrieved or analysed.
- The current client performs basic JSON extraction but does not enforce a complete typed schema or validate every generated field.
- Sift AI is not a replacement for full market research, representative user studies or expert analysis.

The roadmap aims to reduce these limitations through retrieval, provenance tracking, verification and evaluation. Those mechanisms should not be assumed to exist until they are implemented and tested.
